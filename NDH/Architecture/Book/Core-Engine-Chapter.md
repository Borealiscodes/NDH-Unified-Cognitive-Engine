# 📘 **NDH Architecture Book — Core Engine Chapter**  
### *Conceptual Engine • Operational Engine • Interaction Layer • Engine Coherence Protocols*

---

## 🌟 **Chapter Purpose**
The Core Engine Layer defines the **active machinery** of NDH — the systems that perform synthesis, routing, execution, and coherence maintenance.  
Where Governance provides safety, Logic provides processing modes, and the Manifold provides geometry, the Core Engine provides **motion**.

The Core Engine is the **heart** of NDH.

---

## 🧠 **1. Conceptual Engine**  
The Conceptual Engine is responsible for **high‑level cognitive synthesis** — the formation, shaping, and refinement of ideas.

### Responsibilities
- lens selection  
- conceptual synthesis  
- narrative shaping  
- symbolic alignment  
- meaning‑metric evaluation  

### Internal Mechanisms
- **Conceptual Lens Selector**  
  Chooses the correct reasoning, mythic, aesthetic, or symbolic lens.

- **Synthesis Pipeline**  
  Combines multi‑logic inputs into coherent conceptual structures.

- **Narrative Shaper**  
  Applies aesthetic coloration and symbolic motifs.

- **Meaning‑Metric Evaluator**  
  Ensures conceptual output remains stable under SE‑v3.0.

### Cross‑Layer Dependencies
- relies on **Epistemic Logic** for reasoning  
- relies on **Mythic Logic** for archetypal constructs  
- relies on **Manifold Geometry** for spatial placement  

The Conceptual Engine is the **composer** of NDH.

---

## 🔧 **2. Operational Engine**  
The Operational Engine is responsible for **execution** — the actual movement of constructs through NDH’s manifold and logic stacks.

### Responsibilities
- execution pathways  
- stability enforcement  
- logic routing  
- projection control  
- tensor compliance  

### Internal Mechanisms
- **Execution Router**  
  Determines how constructs move through logic stacks.

- **Stability Enforcer**  
  Applies SE‑v3.0 invariants during execution.

- **Projection Controller**  
  Ensures safe dimensional transitions.

- **Tensor Compliance Checker**  
  Validates meaning‑metric, lens‑stress, and intensity invariants.

### Cross‑Layer Dependencies
- relies on **Stability Envelope v3.0** for safety  
- relies on **MLTL‑v1.0** for mythic routing  
- relies on **Manifold Navigation** for traversal  

The Operational Engine is the **executor** of NDH.

---

## 🔗 **3. Engine Interaction Layer**  
The Interaction Layer is the **bridge** between the Conceptual and Operational Engines.  
It ensures that conceptual synthesis and operational execution remain coherent.

### Responsibilities
- conceptual ↔ operational alignment  
- coherence protocols  
- cross‑engine synchronization  
- manifold placement consistency  
- logic‑stack routing integrity  

### Internal Mechanisms
- **Coherence Protocol Manager**  
  Ensures conceptual structures remain stable during execution.

- **Cross‑Engine Synchronizer**  
  Aligns conceptual intent with operational behavior.

- **Dimensional Consistency Checker**  
  Ensures manifold placement matches conceptual geometry.

### Why This Layer Exists
Without the Interaction Layer:

- conceptual output could misalign with execution  
- manifold traversal could contradict logic routing  
- mythic constructs could destabilize reasoning  
- tensor invariants could be violated  

The Interaction Layer is the **glue** of NDH.
