## ⭐ **Overview**

The Nine‑Axis Human‑Dignity Lineage is NDH’s earliest moral architecture — a map of nine human capacities that illuminate how people remain human under pressure.  
Each axis is anchored by a figure whose life or writing demonstrates a distinct form of dignity, clarity, or courage.  
Together, they form a constellation: not of stars, but of **human beings who showed what it means to stay human when the world forgets itself**.

This document defines each axis, its gift, its vector, and its role within the lineage constellation.

---

**Gift:** Conscience that refuses to bend  
**Vector:** Truth spoken even when it costs everything  
**Lineage Function:** Protects moral clarity under threat
