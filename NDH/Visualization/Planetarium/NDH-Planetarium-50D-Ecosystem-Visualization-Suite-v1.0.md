### NDH Planetarium 50D Ecosystem Visualization & Mapping Suite v1.0  
#### *With Bill Nye Tile Teaching Courses*

---

#### 1. Purpose

The **Planetarium Suite** is NDH’s visualization layer for its 50D dimensional ecosystems.  
It lets you *see* the manifold, tensor fields, epochs, and bifurcation safety—without ever turning visualization into architecture or letting anime bleed into the core system.

It also includes **Bill Nye Tile Teaching Courses**: small, self‑contained tiles that teach each concept in kid‑friendly, lab‑coat style while staying mathematically honest.

---

#### 2. Core components

- **Planetarium Engine**  
  Renders 2D/3D/abstract projections of slices of the 50D manifold \(M_{NDH}\).

- **Tensor Overlay System**  
  Shows concept, ethics, stability, creativity, and meta tensors as layered visual fields.

- **Epoch & Inflection Mapper**  
  Visualizes inflection points, epochs, and evolution trajectories.

- **Bifurcation Safety Shell**  
  Explicit visual boundary showing where anime/narrative manifolds are *observed* but never embedded.

- **Bill Nye Tile Teaching Courses**  
  Tile‑based explainer modules that walk through each part of the system.

---

#### 3. Planetarium layers

**3.1 Manifold Layer**

- **What it shows:**  
  - Regions of the 50D manifold projected into viewable slices.  
  - Curvature heatmaps from the Tensor Curvature Map.  
  - Stability fields \(s(x)\) as gradients.

- **Rules:**  
  - Only NDH‑native tensors.  
  - No narrative manifolds rendered as structural layers.

---

**3.2 Tensor Overlay Layer**

- **Concept overlay:** semantic terrain, adjacency, lenses.  
- **Creativity overlay:** harmonic waves, branching paths.  
- **Ethics overlay:** safe/unsafe zones (unsafe only as “blocked” regions).  
- **Stability overlay:** smooth vs turbulent regions.  
- **Meta overlay:** governance and audit zones.

Each overlay is a **visualization** of tensors already defined in the 50D Tensor Field Registry—no new tensors are created here.

---

**3.3 Epoch & Inflection Layer**

- Marks **inflection points** where evolution curves change sign or direction.  
- Shows **epochs** as bands or rings around the manifold timeline.  
- Lets you scrub through “before/after” states of tensor evolution.

This layer is driven by the Master Equation v2.0 and the Stability Audit, but never modifies them—only reads and displays.

---

#### 4. Bifurcation Safety Visualization

The Planetarium explicitly visualizes the **Bifurcation Layer**:

- Anime/RPG worlds appear in a **separate outer shell**.  
- Lines from NDH to anime are labeled **“view‑only”**.  
- Any attempt to embed anime tensors is shown as a **red‑flag animation** and marked “architecturally invalid”.

Visually, it teaches:

> “You can *look* at anime storms,  
> but NDH lives behind the safety glass.”

---

#### 5. Bill Nye Tile Teaching Courses

Each **Tile Course** is a small, modular teaching unit:

- **Tile 1: “What’s a 50D Ecosystem?”**  
  - Explains the manifold as a planetarium of ideas.

- **Tile 2: “Meet the Tensor Squad”**  
  - Concept, Creativity, Ethics, Stability, Meta—each as a character, but grounded in real tensor roles.

- **Tile 3: “Safety Goggles: Tensor Invariants”**  
  - Norm bounds, curvature limits, stability gradients, ethical non‑negativity.

- **Tile 4: “The Plexiglass Wall: Bifurcation Layer”**  
  - Why anime is visual‑only, not structural.

- **Tile 5: “Epochs & Inflection Points”**  
  - How curves change, how systems evolve safely.

- **Tile 6: “Build a World, Don’t Break One”**  
  - Guided worldbuilding using only NDH‑native tensors and safety rules.

Each tile:

- references the real NDH artifacts (Master Spec, Safety Envelope, Curvature Map, etc.)  
- uses metaphors, but **never** lets metaphors override the math.
