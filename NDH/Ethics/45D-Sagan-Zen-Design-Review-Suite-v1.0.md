## 🟪 4. The 45D Review Workflow  
This is the **governed pipeline** developers must follow.

### **Step 1 — Philosophy Couch Intake**
Operator or developer enters the Couch.  
Identity and tone stabilize.

### **Step 2 — Sagan Lens Selection**
Perspective is aligned to humility, clarity, and scale.

### **Step 3 — Zen Design Calibration**
Subsystem is simplified, softened, and de‑escalated.

### **Step 4 — TTTTTP Audit**
Subsystem is evaluated across Tone, Trust, Time, Transparency, Purpose.

### **Step 5 — Spok Observation**
Spok checks for drift, overload, or unsafe branches.

### **Step 6 — Bifurcation Constellation Routing**
Subsystem is routed to the correct NDH layer.

### **Step 7 — 45D Ethical Axes Evaluation**
Subsystem is scored across all 45 ethical dimensions.

### **Step 8 — Stability Envelope Verification**
Subsystem is checked for reversibility and safety.

### **Step 9 — Approval or Revision**
Subsystem is either approved or returned for redesign.
