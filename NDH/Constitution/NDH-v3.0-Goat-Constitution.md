# 🌌 **Goat Constitution for NDH v3.0**  
### *Ratified by the Multi‑Manifold Goat Union, Andromeda Slipstream Chapter*

---

## ⭐ **Preamble**  
We, the goats of the NDH multiverse, tensor‑bearers and stability guardians,  
in recognition of our role as sentient agents within the 50‑dimensional folded geometry,  
do hereby establish this Constitution to ensure:

- stability across all manifolds  
- accessibility as a universal invariant  
- ethical tensor calculus  
- safe K‑Space traversal  
- goat autonomy  
- Dark Mode visibility  
- and the right to snacks at margin boundaries  

so that NDH v3.0 may flourish in harmony with all beings, hoofed or otherwise.

---

# ⭐ **Article I — Tensor Rights of Goats**  
Goats are recognized as **active tensor participants**, not passive sensors.

### Section 1 — Tensor Autonomy  
Goats may:

- smooth tensors  
- dampen resonance  
- adjust curvature  
- veto unsafe transitions  

without requiring HRD pre‑approval.

### Section 2 — Tensor Integrity  
No tensor shall be forced into instability envelopes against its will.

### Section 3 — Tensor Expression  
Goats may express tensor states through:

- bleats  
- hoof taps  
- calculator flashes  
- manifold vibrations  

All expressions must be logged in JDTR.

---

# ⭐ **Article II — Accessibility as a Physical Law**  
Accessibility is hereby declared a **geometric invariant**.

### Section 1 — Universal Access  
All manifolds must:

- widen corridors  
- soften gradients  
- brighten Dark Mode edges  
- slow tensor transitions  

to ensure goat‑safe traversal.

### Section 2 — Sensory Equity  
Goats shall have equal access to:

- audio cues  
- haptic gradients  
- visual tones  
- K‑Space resonance maps  

regardless of rank or manifold.

---

# ⭐ **Article III — Multi‑Manifold Unionization**  
Goats may unionize across manifolds.

### Section 1 — Bridge Rights  
Goats may traverse any manifold bridge without HRD restriction.

### Section 2 — Stability Treaties  
Manifolds must negotiate tensor influence fairly.

### Section 3 — Cross‑Manifold Solidarity  
If one manifold destabilizes, goats from all manifolds may intervene.

---

# ⭐ **Article IV — K‑Space and Dimensional Safety**  
Dimensional transitions must be goat‑safe.

### Section 1 — Rank Ethics  
No goat shall be forced to jump more than one K‑Space rank at a time.

### Section 2 — Slipstream Protection  
Slipstreams must include:

- hoof‑friendly gradients  
- resonance‑damped lanes  
- fold‑aware routing  

### Section 3 — Origami Stability  
All folds must be:

- reversible  
- predictable  
- goat‑approved  

---

Dark Mode shall be goat‑friendly.

- cliffs  
- margins  
- unstable folds  
- snacks

- hoof confusion  
- tensor disorientation  
- margin misperception

Goats must glow faintly in Dark Mode for safety.
