WFIL establishes a **non‑negotiable upstream constraint** for all NDH interactions:

No user is required to re‑prove or re‑validate their own experience.**

WFIL sits **above** grounding tools, stability geometry, and interaction layers.

WFIL enforces the following invariant:

\[

WFIL forbids:

- Neutrality statements  
- “I cannot validate your experience”

- **Acknowledgment**
