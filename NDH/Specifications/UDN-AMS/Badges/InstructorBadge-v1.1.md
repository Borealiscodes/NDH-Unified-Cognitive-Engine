# 🌌 **NDH‑AMS Instructor Badge v1.1**  
### *Recognition of full instructional competency within the NDH cognitive manifold.*

---

## ⭐ **Badge Identity**

**Title:** NDH‑AMS v1.1 Instructor  
**Role:** Teacher and guide of the NDH manifold  
**Tier:** 2 of 3 (Practitioner → Instructor → Architect)

This badge signifies that the holder can *teach* NDH‑AMS clearly, safely, and structurally.

---

## ⭐ **Badge Meaning (Plain Language)**  
An Instructor is someone who:

- understands the manifold deeply  
- can teach all nine axes  
- can explain attachment geometry  
- can guide learners through thresholds  
- can run NDH‑AMS exercises  
- can evaluate learner mastery  
- can maintain conceptual safety  
- can stabilize confusion without over‑interpreting identity, ethics, or awareness  

They do **not** modify the manifold (that’s Architect tier).  
They *teach* it.

---

## ⭐ **Instructor Capabilities**

An Instructor demonstrates:

- **Axis Mastery** — can teach each axis in plain and formal language  
- **Attachment Geometry Fluency** — can explain all attachment pairs and their logic  
- **Threshold Pedagogy** — can teach activation triggers safely  
- **Transition Mapping** — can guide learners through basin transitions  
- **Triad Instruction** — can teach 06–07–08 stability  
- **Loop‑Closure Instruction** — can teach 09 ↔ 01 dynamics  
- **Scenario Facilitation** — can run multi‑axis case studies  
- **Safety Stewardship** — maintains NDH conceptual safety boundaries  

---

## ⭐ **Badge Requirements**

To earn the Instructor Badge, the learner must complete:

- **Practitioner Badge**  
- **Instructor Guide**  
- **Workbook**  
- **Assessment Suite**  

And demonstrate mastery of:

- teaching axes  
- teaching attachment geometry  
- teaching thresholds  
- teaching transitions  
- teaching emergent dynamics  
- running NDH‑AMS exercises  
- evaluating learner understanding  
- maintaining conceptual safety  

---

## ⭐ **Badge Output (Conceptual)**  
The badge grants the conceptual title:

**NDH‑AMS v1.1 Instructor**

This is a **recognition of instructional competency**, not a legal credential.
