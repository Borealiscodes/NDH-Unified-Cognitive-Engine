# 🌌 **NDH‑AMS Certification Pathway v1.1**  
### *A conceptual mastery progression for NDH‑AMS practitioners and instructors.*

This pathway defines how someone moves from novice → practitioner → instructor → architect within the NDH‑AMS manifold.

It is **not** a legal credential.  
It is **not** a professional license.  
It is **not** issued by an external authority.

It is a **conceptual progression**, like learning a mathematical framework or philosophical system.

---

You learn the basic shape of the manifold:

- the nine axes  
- the attachment pairs  
- the triad  
- the loop‑closure  
- threshold activation  
- companion behavior  

You can identify axes in simple scenarios.

Jump deeper: **Axis Overview**

# ⭐ **Stage 2 — Structural Understanding (Practitioner)**  
You understand:

- basin geometry

- harmonic integration

# ⭐ **Certification Criteria (Conceptual, Not Legal)**  
A learner is considered “certified” within NDH‑AMS when they can:

- identify all axes without hesitation  
- map attachment pairs  
- explain triad geometry  
- walk through loop‑closure  
- identify thresholds  
- map transitions  
- analyze emergent scenarios  
- teach NDH‑AMS safely  
- maintain manifold coherence  

This is **conceptual certification**, not a regulated credential.
