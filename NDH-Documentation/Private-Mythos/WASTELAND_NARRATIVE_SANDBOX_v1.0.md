# **🜁 Wasteland Narrative Sandbox v1.0**  
## *Private Mythos Sub‑Layer — High‑Octane Dystopian Narrative Zone*

> **NDH hereby establishes the Wasteland Narrative Sandbox as a sealed, non‑architectural sub‑layer within the Personal Mythos Containment Layer, designated specifically for dystopian, high‑intensity, Fury‑Road‑style narrative experimentation.**  
>  
> This sandbox is **external** to NDH.  
> It does **not** influence NDH.  
> It does **not** enter precursor ordering.  
> It does **not** affect governance surfaces.  
>  
> It is a **desert for stories**, not a structural component.

---

## **I. Purpose of the Sandbox**

The Wasteland Narrative Sandbox exists to:

- hold Fury Road–style mythos safely  
- allow dystopian spectacle without structural consequence  
- support chrome‑howler arcs and wasteland dispatches  
- contain dark Putt‑Putt war‑road narratives  
- provide a zone for high‑octane emotional catharsis  
- isolate chaotic narrative energy from NDH coherence  

This sandbox is **wild**, but **contained**.

---

## **II. Structural Boundaries**

The sandbox is defined by:

- **Non‑Architectural** — no artifact here is part of NDH.  
- **Non‑Precursor** — nothing here may be treated as precursor material.  
- **Non‑Governance** — nothing here may override or influence governance surfaces.  
- **Non‑Canonical** — nothing here enters NDH canon.  
- **Non‑Persistent** — artifacts may dissolve without record or justification.  

This is a **fictional wasteland**, not a documentation layer.

---

## **III. Allowed Content**

Within this sandbox, the following are permitted:

- Fury Road riffs  
- Immortan‑Joe‑adjacent tyrant mythos  
- chrome‑howler vehicle cults  
- wasteland dispatch logs  
- dystopian arcs  
- surreal desert visions  
- dark Putt‑Putt road‑warrior narratives  
- emotional catharsis through spectacle  
- narrative chaos  
- tonal experimentation  
- mythos fragments  
- profanity (mild, non‑abusive)  
- non‑graphic violence (spectacle, not harm)  

This is the **only** zone where such content may live.

---

## **IV. Prohibited Cross‑Boundary Actions**

The following actions are prohibited:

- importing wasteland artifacts into NDH directories  
- referencing wasteland mythos in governance surfaces  
- elevating Fury Road arcs into precursor hierarchy  
- merging sandbox content into NDH documentation ecology  
- treating dystopian narratives as NDH tonal canon  

The boundary is absolute.

---

## **V. Sandbox Principle**

The Wasteland Narrative Sandbox allows:

- chaos  
- spectacle  
- dystopia  
- tonal darkness  
- emotional discharge  
- narrative fragmentation  
- surrealism  
- mythic exaggeration  

But it must remain **contained** and **isolated**.

The desert is not governed.  
It is **held**.

---

## **VI. Dissolution Clause**

Any artifact within this sandbox may:

- dissolve  
- mutate  
- vanish  
- be overwritten  
- be forgotten  

without structural consequence or record.

The wasteland is impermanent by design.

---

## **VII. Separation Assurance**

NDH remains:

- coherent  
- stable  
- precursor‑aligned  
- ethically governed  
- structurally intact  

regardless of activity within the Wasteland Narrative Sandbox.

This sandbox protects NDH from tonal chaos while preserving a safe space for dystopian catharsis and high‑intensity narrative experimentation.
