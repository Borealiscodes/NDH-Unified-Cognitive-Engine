## **I. Purpose**

This companion explains **how NDH runs**, step by step, without mathematical notation.  
It describes:

- what the kernel updates  
- how the manifold evolves  
- how domains and entities behave  
- how governance and policy intervene  
- how transport and interaction occur  
- how axioms expand  

It is the “human‑readable” version of the **Simulation & Execution Protocol**.

---

# **II. The Unified State (Conceptual)**

At any moment, NDH holds a single unified state:

### **The NDH State Contains:**

- **Geometry** — the shape and curvature of the manifold  
- **Fields** — serenity, resonance, paradox, appateur, semantic  
- **Stability** — how safe or risky each region is  
- **Governance** — regulatory influence across the manifold  
- **Channels** — appateur‑mediated non‑local connections  
- **Domains** — structured regions with distinct field signatures  
- **Entities** — organisms formed from field interactions  
- **Axioms** — foundational semantic rules  
- **Policies** — active governance strategies  

Everything NDH does is an update to this state.

---

# **III. The Kernel Step (Math‑Light Version)**

The kernel advances NDH by performing **ten conceptual phases**.  
Think of it as a cosmic “tick.”

```
NDH Step:
1. Update geometry
2. Update fields
3. Update domains
4. Update entities
5. Update interactions & transport
6. Audit stability
7. Update governance
8. Evaluate policy
9. Dispatch actions
10. Update axioms & finalize state
```

Each phase is described below.

---

# **IV. Phase 1 — Geometry Update**

The manifold’s shape changes slightly each step.

- gentle curvature shifts  
- new ridges or wells forming  
- geometry responding to field activity  
- corridors stretching or relaxing  

Geometry is the “terrain” NDH runs on.

---

# **V. Phase 2 — Field Evolution**

The five NDH fields evolve:

- **Serenity** stabilizes regions  
- **Resonance** amplifies activity  
- **Paradox** destabilizes and mutates regions  
- **Appateur** creates non‑local connectivity  
- **Semantic** governs meaning and operator behavior  

Fields influence each other, creating emergent patterns.

---

# **VI. Phase 3 — Domain Update**

Domains are recalculated:

- new domains may form  
- existing domains drift  
- boundaries shift  
- unstable domains may collapse  
- collapsed domains may seed new ones  

Domains are NDH’s “ecosystems.”

---

# **VII. Phase 4 — Entity Update**

Entities behave according to their ecological roles:

- stabilizers calm regions  
- amplifiers intensify resonance  
- disruptors spread paradox  
- mediators manage appateur channels  
- shifters adaptively change roles  

Entities migrate, evolve, or vanish depending on domain conditions.

---

# **VIII. Phase 5 — Interaction & Transport**

This is where NDH becomes dynamic:

- resonance corridors carry influence  
- paradox fronts move across regions  
- appateur channels connect distant areas  
- semantic flux shifts meaning across the manifold  
- cross‑domain transport redistributes energy and influence  

This is the “weather system” of NDH.

---

# **IX. Phase 6 — Stability Audit**

NDH checks for danger:

- collapsing domains  
- overloaded corridors  
- paradox storms  
- unstable governance regions  
- unsafe channel networks  

If instability is detected, governance and policy will intervene.

---

# **X. Phase 7 — Governance Update**

Governance adjusts:

- authority flows across domains  
- regulation strengthens or weakens  
- governance nodes activate or deactivate  
- cross‑domain governance becomes coherent or fragmented  

Governance is the “immune system” of NDH.

---

# **XI. Phase 8 — Policy Evaluation**

Policies examine the current state and decide what to do:

- stabilize  
- amplify  
- reroute  
- dampen  
- collapse  
- merge  
- split  
- regulate  
- expand  

Policies produce **decision tensors**, but here we treat them conceptually as **actions**.

---

# **XII. Phase 9 — Action Dispatch**

Actions are applied:

- geometry is adjusted  
- fields are strengthened or weakened  
- operators are throttled or amplified  
- appateurs are rerouted  
- domains are stabilized or collapsed  
- entities change roles  
- governance pressure is redistributed  

This is NDH’s “executive function.”

---

# **XIII. Phase 10 — Axiom Update**

NDH’s foundational rules evolve:

- new axioms may form  
- axioms may merge or split  
- cross‑axiom transport may increase  
- semantic rules may shift  

This is NDH’s “cosmological evolution.”
