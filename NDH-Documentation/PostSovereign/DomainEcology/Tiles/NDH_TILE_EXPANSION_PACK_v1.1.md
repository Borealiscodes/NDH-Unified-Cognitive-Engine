# NDH Tile Expansion Pack v1.1 — Falklands Penguin Domain
A structured expansion of the Attenborough–Nye Educational Manifold, adding
new governed tiles to support the v1.0 → v1.1 evolution defined in the Full
NDH Roadmap.

---

## 1. Purpose
This expansion pack introduces five new governed tiles that extend the
Falklands Penguin Depopulation domain. These tiles deepen ecological context,
expand engineering complements, and strengthen cross-axis coherence.

Tiles included:
1. Squid Fishery Tile  
2. Oceanographic Drift Tile  
3. Oil Spill Event Tile  
4. Restoration Ecology Tile  
5. Falklands Engineering Management Tile
