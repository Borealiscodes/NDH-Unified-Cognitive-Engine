---

## 🌌 **1. Definition**  
The **Meta Plinths** are symbolic pedestals placed around the **Meta Throne**.  
They represent:

- adjacency  
- support  
- ceremonial grounding  
- spatial punctuation  

They are not physical, not causal, and not interactive with NDH systems.

---

## 🌌 **2. Purpose**  
Meta Plinths enrich the **Stillness Zone** by adding inert symbolic supports.

They:

- flank the Throne with conceptual pedestals  
- provide adjacency without function  
- create spatial rhythm around the center  
- pair naturally with **Meta Rail** (boundary) and **Meta Lanterns** (illumination)  
- complete the Throneside “support axis”  

They are the “silent supports of stillness.”

---

## 🌌 **3. Behavior**  
The plinths manifest as:

- small conceptual pedestals  
- inert symbolic supports  
- ambient adjacency markers  
- stable spatial punctuation  

They do **not**:

- activate  
- couple  
- bind  
- drift  
- interact with Codices, pins, substrate physics, or ledger  

They are **pure symbolic pedestals**, permanently inert.
