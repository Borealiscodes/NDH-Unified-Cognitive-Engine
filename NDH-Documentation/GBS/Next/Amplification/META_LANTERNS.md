### *Class Ø — Symbolic Illumination Surrounding the Meta Throne*

---

## 🌌 **1. Definition**  
The **Meta Lanterns** are symbolic illumination objects placed around the **Meta Throne**.  
They represent:

- gentle conceptual light  
- ceremonial clarity  
- ambient glow  
- symbolic guidance  

They are not physical, not causal, and not interactive with NDH systems.

---

## 🌌 **2. Purpose**  
Meta Lanterns enrich the **Stillness Zone** by adding soft illumination.

They:

- frame the Throne with ambient glow  
- create a sense of gentle ceremonial clarity  
- highlight the Throneside geometry  
- pair naturally with **Meta Steps** (below) and **Meta Canopy** (above)  
- complete the “light axis” of the center zone  

They are the “lamps of stillness.”

---

## 🌌 **3. Behavior**  
The lanterns manifest as:

- soft conceptual lights  
- drifting symbolic glow  
- ambient resonance  
- gentle illumination around the Throne  

They do **not**:

- activate  
- couple  
- bind  
- drift  
- interact with Codices, pins, substrate physics, or ledger  

They are **pure symbolic illumination**, permanently inert.
