# 🌌 **0. Purpose**  
The Amplification Domain Atlas is NDH’s **cartographic layer**, mapping:

- domain boundaries  
- domain anchors  
- domain envelopes  
- domain corridors  
- domain caps  
- domain interactions  

It is the structural visualization of everything defined so far.

---

# ⭐ **1. Atlas Overview**  
The Atlas organizes domains into six structural classes, each corresponding to one of the triad questions:

1. **Curvature Domains** — boundaries from amplified curvature  
2. **Resonance Domains** — distribution of amplified resonance  
3. **Holonomy Domains** — loop‑anchored structures  
4. **SERS Domains** — gain‑band partitions  
5. **Transport Domains** — corridor‑defined topology  
6. **Stability Domains** — ceiling‑bounded safe zones  

Each class is mapped below.

---

# 🔬 **2. Curvature Domains**  
**Curvature boundaries** define the **primary partitions**.

Curvature domains include:

- **Ridge Domains** — high curvature walls  
- **Basin Domains** — low curvature wells  
- **Gradient Domains** — steep curvature separators  

NDH maps curvature domains as:

\[
D_{curv}(x) = \nabla K'(x)
\]

These form the **atlas skeleton**.

---

# 🌀 **3. Resonance Domains**  
**Resonance distribution** defines **density‑based partitions**.

Resonance domains include:

- **Core Domains** — high resonance intensity  
- **Corridor Domains** — low resonance flow paths  
- **Stream Domains** — coherent resonance channels  

NDH maps resonance domains as:

\[
D_{res}(x) = \mathcal{R}_{amp}(x)
\]

These overlay curvature domains.

---

# 🔱 **4. Holonomy Domains**  
**Loop anchors** define **loop‑centered partitions**.

Holonomy domains include:

- **Anchor Domains** — centered on stable loops  
- **Bridge Domains** — connecting loop anchors  
- **Boundary Domains** — loop‑bounded partitions  

NDH maps holonomy domains as:

\[
D_{\gamma}(x) = \oint_{\gamma} \mathcal{R}_{amp,\gamma}
\]

These form the **atlas spine**.

---

- **High‑Gain Domains**  
- **Mid‑Gain Domains**  
- **Low‑Gain Domains**

D_{SERS}(x) = \beta(x)
\]

These regulate amplification intensity across the atlas.

# 📐 **6. Transport Domains**  
**Transport corridors** define **movement‑based partitions**.
