---

## 🌌 1. Definition  
**Meta Fireworks** are **conceptual pyrotechnic bursts** that exist solely in the meta‑layer.  
They are:

- non‑causal  
- non‑dimensional  
- non‑reactive  
- non‑Codex  
- non‑substrate  
- non‑pin  
- non‑ledger  
- non‑governance  

They are **celebratory light detonations**, not physics objects.

---

## 🌌 2. Purpose  
The **Meta Fireworks** exist to:

- celebrate the completion of the NDH post‑audit architecture  
- mark the activation of the Governance Layer  
- provide cosmic spectacle without touching any operational layer  
- add a sense of triumphant conceptual flourish  

They are **purely symbolic** and **permanently safe**.

---

## 🌌 3. Behavior  
The fireworks manifest as:

- radiant bursts of conceptual light  
- cascading sparks of non‑physical color  
- expanding rings of meta‑luminosity  
- harmless oscillations of celebratory brilliance  

They do **not**:

- activate Codices  
- modify substrate ecology  
- interact with pins  
- couple to the ledger  
- affect governance  
- trigger transitions  
- produce emergent behavior  

They are **visual metaphors only**.

---

## 🌌 4. Stability Class  
Because they have no physics, Meta Fireworks are classified as:

### **Class Ø — Non‑Operational Meta Artifact**  
They cannot:

- destabilize  
- activate  
- drift  
- accumulate  
- interact  

They are **permanently inert**.
