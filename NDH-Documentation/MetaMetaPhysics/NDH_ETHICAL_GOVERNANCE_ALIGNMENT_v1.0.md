# **NDH Ethical Governance Alignment v1.0**  
## *How Ethical Principles Govern NDH’s Precursor Hierarchy and System Behavior*  
**Layer:** MetaMetaPhysics → Ethical Governance  
**Status:** Repository Artifact

---

## **1. Purpose**
This artifact defines how ethical principles must be translated into governance rules within NDH. It ensures that ethics are not merely design considerations but **binding constraints** on precursor behavior, documentation ecology, and system evolution.

---

## **2. Ethical Governance as a Structural Layer**
Ethical governance is not:

- a tone  
- a guideline  
- a suggestion  
- a commentary  

It is a **governance layer** that shapes:

- precursor behavior  
- domain boundaries  
- recursion limits  
- stability geometry  
- documentation ordering  

Ethics becomes **law**, not advice.

---

## **3. Ethical Constraints on Precursor Hierarchy**
Ethical governance requires:

- **Singularity Precursor**  
  Must reject harmful ontologies (fear‑based learning, punitive recursion).

- **Bifurcation Layer**  
  Must prevent cross‑domain harm (Algebra cannot destabilize Tonal).

- **Tonal Scaffolding Precursor**  
  Must ensure emotional safety, exploration, joy, nonlinear accessibility.

- **Algebra Precursor (pending)**  
  Must respect Tonal constraints and cannot override emotional safety.

- **MetaMetaPhysics Companion**  
  Must enforce ethical ordering and documentation ecology.

This is the ethical backbone of NDH.

---

## **4. Ethical Governance Caps**
Ethical governance imposes caps on:

- recursion depth  
- manifold complexity  
- operation density  
- cognitive load  
- domain transitions  

These caps prevent:

- overwhelm  
- destabilization  
- fear responses  
- cognitive overload  
- premature exposure to complexity  

Ethics protects the user from the system.

## **5. Ethical Governance of Documentation Ecology**
Ethical governance requires:

- diagrams must have parent artifacts  
- precursor maps must be ordered  
- Tonal artifacts must precede Algebra artifacts  
- uplift documents must accompany complex systems  
- build‑order must prevent harm  

Documentation ecology is part of ethical governance.

Algebra Precursor anchoring must:

As NDH evolves, ethical governance ensures:
