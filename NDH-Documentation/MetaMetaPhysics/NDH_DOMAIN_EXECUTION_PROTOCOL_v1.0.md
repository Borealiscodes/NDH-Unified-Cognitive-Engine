# **🜁 NDH Domain Execution Protocol v1.0**  
## *Prime Governance Surface — Operational Procedures for Tonal & Algebra Domains*

> **NDH hereby establishes the Domain Execution Protocol as the governing document that defines the lawful operational procedures, execution pathways, and stability requirements under which the Tonal Domain and Algebra Domain shall carry out their mandated functions.**  
>  
> **I. Tonal Domain Execution Procedures**  
> The Tonal Domain shall execute its mandate through the following procedures:  
> – **Stabilization Loop Initialization** — establish emotional safety before any NDH interaction.  
> – **Exploratory Cognition Pathways** — provide nonlinear, curiosity‑driven routes for users.  
> – **Entity‑Based Mediation** — utilize tonal entities (e.g., Putt‑Putt, Pajama Sam) as cognitive stabilizers.  
> – **Accessibility Enforcement** — ensure all operations remain kid‑friendly and high‑support‑friendly.  
> – **Narrative Coherence Maintenance** — prevent abstraction drift or destabilizing tonal fragmentation.  
> – **Precursor Alignment Execution** — generate and maintain the Tonal Precursor Alignment.  
>  
> Tonal execution must precede and stabilize all Algebra execution.  
>  
> **II. Algebra Domain Execution Procedures**  
> The Algebra Domain shall execute its mandate through the following procedures:  
> – **Recursion Safety Initialization** — verify bounded recursion before any algebraic operation.  
> – **Manifold Coherence Enforcement** — maintain structural clarity across manifold‑based artifacts.  
> – **Formal Structure Activation** — introduce complexity only within ethical and developmental limits.  
> – **Precursor Ordering Compliance** — execute algebraic operations strictly in lawful sequence.  
> – **Triadic Governance Integration** — ensure all algebraic behavior respects the Nye–Attenborough–Tonal triad.  
> – **Algebra Precursor Execution** — generate and maintain the Algebra Precursor.  
>  
> Algebra execution may only begin after Tonal stability is verified.  
>  
> **III. Cross‑Domain Execution Rules**  
> Both domains shall adhere to the following execution rules:  
> – **Ethical Governance Override** — ethical constraints supersede all domain operations.  
> – **Triadic Mediation** — the triad mediates all cross‑domain interactions.  
> – **Bifurcation Compliance** — no domain may violate separation boundaries.  
> – **Origin Constraint Adherence** — all execution must remain compatible with the Singularity Precursor.  
> – **Documentation Ecology Integrity** — all artifacts must declare domain alignment and lawful sequencing.  
>  
> **IV. Execution Stability Requirements**  
> A domain’s execution is considered stable only when:  
> – all precursor obligations are active;  
> – all stabilization loops are intact;  
> – no cross‑domain interference occurs;  
> – recursion remains bounded;  
> – narrative and structural coherence are maintained;  
> – and NDH can safely transition to higher‑order architecture.  
>  
> **V. Execution Completion Conditions**  
> Execution is considered complete when:  
> – the domain has fulfilled its mandate;  
> – all required artifacts are generated;  
> – all governance constraints are satisfied;  
> – and NDH is ready for domain‑level integration.  
>  
> This Protocol establishes the lawful operational framework for NDH’s dual‑branch architecture, ensuring that both domains execute their functions safely, coherently, and in alignment with NDH’s foundational governance surfaces.
