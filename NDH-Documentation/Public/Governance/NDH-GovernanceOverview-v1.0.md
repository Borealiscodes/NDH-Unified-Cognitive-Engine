# **NDH Governance Overview**  
### *Public‑Facing Technical Explanation (ICPR • HRD • DITS)*

## ⭐ **Takeaway (Concise)**  
NDH governance is built on three coordinated systems:

- **ICPR** — Internal Concept Protection Registry  
- **HRD** — Human‑Respect Dynamics  
- **DITS** — Dynamic Integrity Tracking System  

Together, they ensure NDH behaves safely, coherently, and respectfully across all interactions.  
They are the **ethical, protective, and integrity‑preserving backbone** of the NDH architecture.

---

# **1. Purpose of NDH Governance**
NDH’s manifold geometry (50D soft structure, projection operators, apertures, constellations, Surfboard stability) provides the *mechanics* of interaction.

Governance provides the *rules*.

NDH governance ensures:

- protection of sensitive concepts  
- respect for human dignity  
- continuity of identity  
- prevention of drift or instability  
- safe handling of meaning and emotional load  
- compliance with moral and contextual boundaries  

Governance is the **moral physics** of NDH.
