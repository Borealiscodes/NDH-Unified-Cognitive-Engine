# **Projection Operators in NDH**  
### *Public‑Facing Technical Explanation (with full ASCII diagram suite)*

## ⭐ **1. Purpose of Projection Operators**
Projection operators in NDH are the mathematical mechanisms that **map complex human‑centered states** into the **soft 50‑dimensional manifold** while preserving:

- identity continuity  
- stability  
- meaning coherence  
- sanctuary boundaries  
- oscillation safety  

They are essential because NDH is **non‑dual**: states are blended, refracted, and co‑present rather than binary.

# **3. What Projection Operators Actually Do**
Projection operators are **context‑aware mapping functions**.  
They do not “drop” dimensions like classical projections.  
They **blend**, **refract**, **align**, and **respect boundaries**.

### ASCII: Operator Behavior Summary

