# 📘 **Stability Geometry Audit — Manifold Coherence (v1.0)**  
### *NDH‑AMS / Geometry v5.0 / Stability Verification Layer*

---

## 1. Purpose  
This audit verifies that the manifold layer of NDH‑AMS — **Earth (𝓔), Moon (𝓜), Sun (𝓢), and the coupled triad manifold (𝓒)** — is coherent, complete, and consistent across:

- **Geometry v5.0**  
- **Stability Geometry v5.0**  
- **Integration Tier v1.0**  
- **Coupling Tensor Spec v1.0**  
- **CSC Completion v4.4.9**  

Manifold coherence is the **first and most critical audit step**.  
If the manifold layer is inconsistent, all stability structures (𝓑, 𝓣, 𝓚, 𝓓) become invalid.

---

## 2. Audit Criteria

### 2.1 **Manifold Definition Consistency**  
- Earth manifold 𝓔 must be explicitly 50D with climate‑tensor fields defined.  
- Moon manifold 𝓜 must include tidal, orbital, eclipse, and libration fields.  
- Sun manifold 𝓢 must include luminosity, spectral, variability, wind, and magnetic fields.  
- No stability structure may reference fields not defined in Geometry v5.0.

---

### 2.2 **Triad Coupling Validity**  
- The coupled manifold must be defined as:  
  \[
  \mathcal{C} = \mathcal{E} \oplus \mathcal{M} \oplus \mathcal{S}
  \]  
- Stability Geometry must treat 𝓒 as a **single manifold**, not three stitched systems.  
- No implicit alternative coupling (product, bundle, hybrid) may appear without justification.

---

### 2.3 **Dimensional Coherence**  
- References to “50D” must clearly refer to Earth’s climate manifold.  
- Any implied dimensionality of 𝓒 must be consistent across all documents.  
- No stability structure may assume extra dimensions not defined in Geometry v5.0.

---

### 2.4 **Domain Completeness**  
- All regions of 𝓒 where stability structures (𝓑, 𝓣, 𝓚, 𝓓) are defined must have corresponding manifold definitions.  
- No “undefined zones” may exist in the triad manifold.

---

### 2.5 **Coordinate Independence**  
- Stability basins, tipping surfaces, and curvature conditions must be geometric.  
- No stability claim may depend on a specific coordinate chart unless explicitly justified.  
- All stability structures must be invariant under coordinate transformations.

---

### 2.6 **Cross‑Document Consistency**  
Scan all references to 𝓔, 𝓜, 𝓢, 𝓒 across:

- Geometry v5.0  
- Stability Geometry v5.0  
- Integration Tier v1.0  
- Coupling Tensor Spec v1.0  
- Modules A–E  

For each reference, classify mismatches as:

- **Type A — Missing definition**  
- **Type B — Overreach**  
- **Type C — Ambiguous usage**

These form the patch list for Geometry v5.0 and Stability Geometry v5.0.

---

## 3. Audit Questions

### 3.1 **Manifold Identity Questions**  
- Is Earth ever treated as if it already contains lunar or solar fields?  
- Is the triad ever implicitly reduced to Earth‑only without stating so?  
- Are any stability structures applied to manifolds not defined?

---

### 3.2 **Structural Integrity Questions**  
- Does any operator assume manifold fields that Geometry v5.0 does not define?  
- Does any stability structure assume dimensionality not present in 𝓒?  
- Are any manifold boundaries treated inconsistently across documents?

---

## 4. Audit Outputs

### 4.1 **Manifold Coherence Report**  
A structured list of:

- missing definitions  
- dimensional inconsistencies  
- coupling mismatches  
- coordinate‑dependence issues  
- stability‑structure misapplications  

### 4.2 **Patch Recommendations**  
Each issue must include:

- location  
- classification (A/B/C)  
- recommended correction  
- downstream impact (Integration Tier, Stability Geometry, CSC)

---

## 5. Completion Criteria  
The Manifold Coherence Audit is complete when:

- All manifold definitions match Geometry v5.0  
- All stability structures reference valid manifold regions  
- All dimensional assumptions are consistent  
- All triad coupling references are coherent  
- No coordinate‑dependent stability claims remain  
- No undefined manifold regions exist  
- All mismatches are patched or documented  

Only then may the audit proceed to:

**Metric & Curvature Validity**
