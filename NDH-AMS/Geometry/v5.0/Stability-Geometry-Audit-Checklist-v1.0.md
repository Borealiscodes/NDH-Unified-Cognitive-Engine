# 📘 **Stability Geometry Audit Checklist v1.0**  
### *NDH‑AMS Geometry v5.0 — Formal Audit Protocol*

---

## 1. **Manifold Coherence**  
- **Manifold definitions:**  
  Verify \( \mathcal{E}, \mathcal{M}, \mathcal{S}, \mathcal{C} \) match Geometry v5.0 exactly.  
- **Dimensional consistency:**  
  Confirm Earth’s 50D climate manifold integrates cleanly with lunar and solar manifolds.  
- **Domain completeness:**  
  Ensure no undefined regions exist in the triad manifold.  
- **Coordinate independence:**  
  Stability claims must be geometric, not coordinate‑dependent.

---

## 2. **Metric & Curvature Validity**  
- **Metric definition:**  
  Confirm \( g_{50} \) is smooth, positive‑definite (or sign‑controlled), and triad‑compatible.  
- **Curvature tensor:**  
  Validate \( \mathcal{K} = \nabla g_{50} \) is well‑posed across all manifolds.  
- **No singularities:**  
  Identify and justify any curvature singularities.  
- **Curvature invariants:**  
  Ensure invariants required by CSC Completion are enforceable.

---

## 3. **Stability Basin Integrity**  
- **Formal definition:**  
  Basins \( \mathcal{B} \) must be defined by explicit geometric conditions.  
- **Topology:**  
  Basins must be connected, bounded, and curvature‑consistent.  
- **Invariance:**  
  Trajectories under CTEO must remain in \( \mathcal{B} \) unless crossing \( \mathcal{T} \).  
- **Operator compatibility:**  
  SFO, LMO, AFO must not fragment basins unintentionally.

---

## 4. **Tipping Surface Precision**  
- **Geometric condition:**  
  Tipping surfaces must satisfy a clear condition (e.g., \( \mathcal{K} = 0 \)).  
- **Codimension‑1:**  
  Verify hypersurface structure.  
- **Continuity:**  
  Ensure no gaps, jumps, or undefined regions.  
- **Non‑artificial origin:**  
  Tipping surfaces must arise from geometry, not arbitrary thresholds.

---

## 5. **Basin Drift Dynamics**  
- **Definition:**  
  Drift \( \mathcal{D}(t) \) must be continuous and differentiable.  
- **No fragmentation:**  
  Drift must not split basins unless explicitly modeled as bifurcation.  
- **Timescale separation:**  
  Fast vs slow drift processes must be distinguished.  
- **Composite forcing:**  
  Drift under \( \Gamma_{\odot} + \Gamma_{\leftmoon} + \Gamma_a \) must remain coherent.

---

## 6. **Curvature Behavior Under Forcing**  
- **Baseline curvature:**

- **Lunar modulation:**  
  Ensure Module B curvature changes are smooth.  
- **Anthropogenic drift:**

  Resonance amplitudes must remain below stability thresholds.
